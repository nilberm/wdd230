Membership Benefits

NP Membership

  • No fee for non-profit organizations
  • Access to networking events
  • Discounted event tickets
  • Opportunity to participate in community initiatives

Bronze Membership

  • Access to networking events
  • Discounted event tickets
  • Listing in the Chamber's online directory
  • Opportunity to participate in committees and task forces

Silver Membership

  • All benefits of Bronze Membership
  • Priority access to exclusive events
  • Enhanced visibility on the Chamber's website
  • Opportunity for sponsorship and advertising opportunities

Gold Membership

  • All benefits of Silver Membership
  • Complimentary event tickets
  • Prominent placement on the Chamber's website
  • Opportunity for thought leadership and speaking engagements
  • Exclusive access to high-level networking events
